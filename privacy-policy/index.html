Privacy Policy

This website is operated by TokyoDev Inc (TokyoDev), a company incorporated and existing under the laws of Japan. When used herein, the terms "we", "us" and "our" refer to TokyoDev.

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information.

Information Collection and Use

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, we collect non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request.

Our purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how our visitors use the website.

From time to time, we may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of our website.

Information about You

To register for our newsletter, we only require an email address. This information is only used to send you the newsletter.

Applying for a job

To apply for jobs via TokyoDev, we ask you to provide a name, email address, message, resume, country of residence, and whether you're willing to relocate to Japan. This information is only used for processing your job application. For the purposes of preventing spam, it may be reviewed by TokyoDev personnel.

Some jobs on TokyoDev link to an external site. This policy doesn't govern information you submit to such a site.

Retention and Deletion

Upon your request, we will remove your personally identifiable information from our active databases as soon as reasonably possible. Nonetheless, we will retain, in our files, information you may have requested us to remove if, in our discretion, retention of the information is necessary to resolve disputes or troubleshoot problems. Furthermore, your information is never completely removed from our databases due to technical and legal constraints (for example, we will not remove your information from our back up storage).

Information Sharing

When you apply for a job via TokyoDev, the information you share with us is passed on directly to the company you are applying for.

When a job on TokyoDev links to an external site, we may include a tracking parameter that helps them identify that your application has come from TokyoDev. We do not share any personal information with them though.

Further, we may disclose your information if we believe that it is reasonably necessary to comply with a law, regulation or legal request; to protect the safety of any person; to address fraud, security or technical issues; or to protect our rights or property.

Besides the points given here, we will not disclose your personal information.

Changes to this Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make a change to this policy that, in our sole discretion, is material, a change notice will be published on the website with an updated effective date. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
